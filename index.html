About Angel Paws

Angel Paws is a non-profit animal rescue and adoption service located in Salt Lake City, Utah.

Project Summary

Our Design team redesigned Angel Paws existing website and created an effective mobile interface and tablet design. Our goal was to identify user frustrations then highlight and improve the most important features which catered to Angel Paws adoption process.

The Problem

Users did not know what their main call to action was when arriving on Angels Paws website. Users did not trust the outdated design. Angel Paws also did not have an adoption gallery on their website which directly affected their adoption process.

The Solution

We created an obvious call to action. We also created a fun, quick, and informative adoption process with updated animal adoption galleries. We also changed the overall tone of the website by updating the UI, ultimately bringing more engagment for users of all ages

Our User Persona helped us visualize and empathize with our potential user.

After identifying our potential users, our team set out to conduct interviews. We crafted an interview plan which focused on questions like: “Have you adopted an animal in the past? How did you feel about that experience?” “What caused you to bring a pet into your life?” “What type of information would you like to know about a pet before adopting?” “Have you used the internet to search for adoptable pets in the past? What did you like and dislike? After conducting interveiws we gained valuable insights into the way our users thought when adopting a pet. These insights really helped us narrow down what our users wanted, what their frustrations were, and how we could be help them during the adoption process.

Problem Statement

Users struggle to navigate and understand the main purpose of Angel Paws' website. It is unclear how to donate, volunteer, and adopt. This confusion results in users leaving the website frustrated, confused, and unable participate in Angel Paws' non-profit organization.
